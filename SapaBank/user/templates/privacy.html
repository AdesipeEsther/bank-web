Sapa Bank Privacy Policy

We at Sapa Bank are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information.

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal and financial information about you, including:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Account numbers, balances, and transaction history
  • Credit and debit card information
  • Identification documents, such as NIN and passports

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information to:

  • Provide you with banking services, such as account management and transactions
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Comply with laws and regulations
  • Improve our services and customer experience

How Do We Share Your Information?

We share your information with:

  • Affiliated companies and subsidiaries
  • Service providers, such as payment processors and data analytics companies
  • Law enforcement and regulatory agencies, as required by law

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We use robust security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption and secure socket layer (SSL) technology
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and testing

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal and financial information
  • Opt out of marketing communications and promotional offers
  • Request that we delete your information

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
